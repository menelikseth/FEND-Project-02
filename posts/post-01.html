This blog post shows a few different types of content that is styled to achieve a consistent viewing experience. The basic layout was roughed-out with mockups that I created using MockPlus. This was the most challenging, and time-consuming, part of the project for me, as I had no prior experience with design software. I went this route because I wanted to give myself a design that I would have to adhere to, so my coding process would not be trial-and-error. I also wanted to challenge myself as much as possible to make the design work. In the end, I had to concede to the limitations of my knowledge, and make some adjustments to simplify the CSS, so that it could adapt to different screen sizes. The mockups can be found here.

I would like to credit Lorem Ipsum for generating the Lorem Ipsum used in this project. I would also like to credit Placehold for generating the placeholder images.

I also want to thank the team behind the Udacity FEND course, for the much needed extension. My move across-state set me back by a month, and without the extension, it would have been impossible for me to catch up.
